How to Create a Nation

Own a Town

You must be a mayor of a town to create a nation.

1000 Money

You need 1000 coins to form a new nation.

5 Residents

Your town must have at least 5 residents.

Command

Use /nation new [Nation Name] to create your nation.

Benefits of Creating a Nation

  • Create alliances with other nations
  • Declare wars and participate in nation battles
  • Manage taxes and create a national bank
  • Set nation ranks and manage permissions
  • Claim bonus chunks for your nation's towns
  • Appear on the server's nations leaderboard
