Got Questions? We Have Answers

Find answers to the most common questions about our server

What is this all about?

We are 3 players that originally came from earthMC, we love the concept and decided to create a server that resemble more like the old earthMC on terra nova.

I want to play right away, what I need to do?

You need to have MC 1.21.4, then you need to go to a continent of your choice with /rtp, then enjoy!

What is the goal of this server?

It's primarily based on the plugin towny, that allows you to create and join town and nation. It allows you to claim chunks to protect these area against folks, so that yourself and you citizens can build what you want, where you want.

What are the command to manage a town?

You can check the command with /t help

How can I claim a chunk?

You need gold and citizens in your town to do so. A chunk cost 16 gold and you got a limit to how much you can claim, you can check it with /t. Once you know you can do /t claim.

How can I gain gold?

There are many ways to earn gold: Mining, selling items to other players, some daily rewards give you gold, try to kill other folks and take their loot.

The server is closed, what is happening?

It may due to different issues likely down for maintenance, check the discord to have additional information.

If I got a problem, whom I can contact?

You can contact us on discord, we got a ticket bot for this purpose.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, feel free to ask in our Discord server or contact our staff team directly.
