Server Rules

Guidelines for a fair and enjoyable experience

Our server is built on respect, fairness, and fun. Following these rules helps us maintain a balanced, respectful, and enjoyable experience for everyone. Violating these rules may result in warnings or bans, depending on the severity and frequency of the offense.

1

Respect All Players

Treat all players with respect. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, and excessive swearing are not tolerated. Keep chat friendly and inclusive for all ages.

2

No Griefing or Stealing

Do not destroy others' property, steal items, or modify builds without permission. Respect town boundaries and claims. All grief will be rolled back and offenders will be punished.

3

No Cheating or Exploits

Using mods that give unfair advantages, exploiting bugs, or using alternate accounts to circumvent rules is prohibited. This includes X-ray texture packs, fly hacks, speed hacks, auto-clickers, etc.

4

Keep Builds Appropriate

All builds must be appropriate for all ages. Offensive, obscene, or inappropriate builds will be removed without warning, and the builder may face penalties.

5

No Spamming

Do not spam the chat with repeated messages, excessive capitalization, or meaningless content. Use the appropriate channels for different types of communication.

6

War and PvP Rules

PvP is only allowed in designated areas or between towns/nations at war. War declarations must follow the proper protocols. No combat logging or excessive spawn killing. Respect truces and peace treaties.

7

Respect Staff Decisions

Staff members have the final say in disputes and rule interpretations. If you disagree with a decision, you may appeal it through proper channels, but do so respectfully and follow the process.

8

Lag Prevention

Do not build machines or farms that cause excessive lag. Redstone contraptions should be optimized, and animal/mob farms should be kept within reasonable limits. Staff may disable or remove lag-causing devices.

9

No Advertising

Do not advertise other servers, websites, or services in chat or through direct messages to players. Relevant Discord servers for towns or nations are allowed with moderation approval.

10

Have Fun!

Remember that Minecraft is a game, and our server is a community. Have fun, be creative, make friends, and enjoy building your towns and nations in our Earth-scale world!

Important Note

These rules may be updated as needed to ensure a fair and enjoyable environment. It is your responsibility to stay informed about the current rules. Staff members have the final authority on rule interpretation and enforcement.

If you witness rule violations, please report them to a staff member via our Discord server rather than confronting players directly.
