Server Rules

Guidelines for a fair and enjoyable experience

Our server is built on respect, fairness, and fun. Following these rules helps us maintain a balanced, respectful, and enjoyable experience for everyone. Violating these rules may result in warnings or bans, depending on the severity and frequency of the offense.

1

Respect Other Players

No Harassment or Bullying: Any form of harassment, bullying, or abusive behavior towards other players is strictly prohibited. Treat others with respect.

2

No Offensive Language

Keep your language appropriate. Avoid using offensive, discriminatory, or explicit language in public chat or private messages.

3

No Griefing

Do not grief the wilderness if not necessary. Stealing is allowed, everyone has to be careful to perms they give to others. No Exploiting: Do not use bugs or glitches to your advantage. Report them immediately to staff.

4

No Hacking or Cheating

No Mods or Cheats: Do not use any modifications, hacks, or cheats that give you an unfair advantage over other players. This includes flying, X-ray, or any form of aimbot. No Client-Side Exploits: Do not use any client-side software that can be used for unfair advantage (e.g., speed hacks, autoclickers, etc.).

5

No Spamming or Advertising

No Spamming in Chat: Do not flood the chat with repetitive messages, capital letters, or irrelevant content. No Advertising: Do not advertise other servers, websites, or services in chat unless given explicit permission by staff.

6

In-Game Behavior

PvP: PvP is allowed in designated areas (e.g., warzones, wilderness). No AFK Farming: Avoid using AFK farms that generate excessive amounts of resources without interaction. These can disrupt the server economy and performance. No Using Unfair Farming Methods: Avoid exploiting automated systems or exploiting farming to an extreme degree (e.g., item duplication).

7

Respect Nations and Cities' Builds

City/Town Territory: Respect the boundaries of other players' cities, towns, or nations. Do not build or mine in their territory without permission. Do Not Block Public Roads: Ensure that your build doesn't obstruct public roads, pathways, or towns unless it is explicitly allowed by the server or town leader.

8

No Multi-Accounting

One Account Per Person: Do not use multiple accounts to gain unfair advantages, such as extra resources or protection. Multi-accounting is prohibited. Family or Shared Devices: If multiple people use the same device, make sure to inform the staff to avoid misunderstandings regarding multiple accounts.

9

Respect Staff and Moderators

Follow Staff Instructions: Always listen to the server staff and moderators. If you feel like you've been treated unfairly, contact an administrator or submit a support ticket. No Arguing with Staff: If you disagree with a decision made by staff, please handle it politely. Arguing in public chat will not resolve the issue.

10

Building and Terrain Rules

No Spamming Structures: Do not create large, unnecessary structures or farms that disrupt the aesthetic or functionality of the server. Terraforming: Do not terraform large areas excessively unless it is a part of your town or nation plan, and be respectful of others' builds.

11

No Inappropriate Content

No Inappropriate Builds: Do not build or display content that is offensive, sexually explicit, or inappropriate for a Minecraft community. No NSFW (Not Safe For Work) Content: Avoid sharing or displaying any inappropriate or adult material, either in-game or in the Discord server.

12

Discord Use

No Toxic Behavior: Just like in the game, we expect respectful behavior on the server's Discord. Treat others with kindness, and avoid toxic interactions. Follow Discord Rules: Ensure you follow both the Discord and Minecraft server rules. Discord is a place for community interaction, so be friendly and help others. No Spamming in Voice Channels: Do not spam voice channels with music bots or disruptive noise. Always respect the other players in the channel.

13

Responsibility for Actions

Your Account is Your Responsibility: You are responsible for all actions taken by your account, whether done by you or someone you've shared your account with. Report Violations: If you see any violations of the rules, please report them to a staff member. Help us maintain a positive and fun community!

Important Note

These rules may be updated as needed to ensure a fair and enjoyable environment. It is your responsibility to stay informed about the current rules. Staff members have the final authority on rule interpretation and enforcement.

If you witness rule violations, please report them to a staff member via our Discord server rather than confronting players directly.

Got Questions About Our Rules?

Join our Discord server for clarification or to report rule violations
